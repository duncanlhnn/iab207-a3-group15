About MusicFestival

MusicFestival is an intuitive and dynamic platform designed to simplify the management and discovery of live music events. The platform connects users with a broad range of concerts and festivals while empowering organizers to create, manage, and share their events online.

Developed using modern web technologies including Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Bootstrap, MusicFestival delivers a smooth and responsive user experience across devices.

Main Features:
  • Browse events by category and status
  • View detailed event pages with images and dates
  • Register and log in to book tickets
  • Leave comments and interact with other users
  • Track personal booking history anytime
  • Create and manage events as a registered user

Whether you're a fan planning your next outing or an organizer launching a new show, MusicFestival offers the tools and flexibility to make it easy, fast, and enjoyable.

This site was developed as part of a web application project using the Flask framework.
